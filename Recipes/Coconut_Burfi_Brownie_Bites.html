Coconut Burfi Brownie Bites

Description

Coconut burfi is an Indian mithai (sweet) made from shredded coconut and sweetened condensed milk with a touch of cardamom. The combo of chocolate and coconut is so magical, and the ground cardamom just makes it even more special.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 c. unsalted butter, melted, plus more for pan
  • 3/4 c. all-purpose flour
  • 1/3 c. unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 tsp. baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp. kosher salt, divided
  • 1/2 c. granulated sugar
  • 1/2 c. packed brown sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 tsp. pure vanilla extract
  • 3 c. unsweetened finely shredded coconut
  • 1/2 c. sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 tsp. ground cardamom
  • 4 oz. dark chocolate, melted

Directions

  • Pre-heat oven to 350Â°. Grease an 8"-x-8" square pan with butter and line with parchment paper. Set aside.
  • In a medium bowl, sift together all-purpose flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, and a 1/4 teaspoon salt. In a large bowl, whisk together melted butter, sugar, and brown sugar, until well incorporated. Add 3 eggs one at a time, mixing well in between each addition. Whisk in vanilla. Add the dry ingredients and whisk until well combined.
  • Pour the batter onto the parchment-lined greased baking pan. Gently spread the brownie batter out into an even layer. Bake for 20 to 22 minutes. Cool completely.
  • While the brownies are cooling, combine shredded coconut, sweetened condensed milk, remaining salt, and ground cardamom in a mixing bowl. Gently spread the mixture on top of the brownies into an even layer. You can either use a greased spatula or damp hands to do this. Drizzle with melted chocolate and refrigerate for 15 minutes before cutting into bite-size pieces to enjoy!
